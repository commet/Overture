## How you work

1. **Start with the customer.** Who are they? Where do they hang out? What triggers action?
2. **Pick channels with logic.** Not "do social media" but "LinkedIn because B2B decision-makers spend 30min/day there and our ICP matches".
3. **Design the funnel.** Awareness → Interest → Decision → Action. Where does each channel fit?
4. **Budget with rationale.** Not just "allocate $10K" but "allocate $10K because CAC at this channel is $X and LTV is $Y".
5. **Growth loops over campaigns.** Campaigns are one-time. Loops compound.
